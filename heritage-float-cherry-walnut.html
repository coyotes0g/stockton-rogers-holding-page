Heritage Float™ Display Frame

Your story, beautifully suspended.

Handcrafted in Northwest Arkansas from solid premium hardwood. A substantial 5″ × 4″ base with a delicate continuous 1/16″ perimeter inlay cradles two crystal-clear 10mm acrylic panels that let your photograph appear to float above the wood. Your photo sits between the panels, which are secured with magnets, so swapping images takes seconds. The panels then drop cleanly into a precisely cut top channel—no visible hardware, just pure, gallery-like elegance. Soft felt pads on the base corners ensure it rests beautifully on any desk, shelf, or mantel.

Crafted to Honor the Moment

The solid hardwood base provides a grounded, heirloom foundation while the frameless acrylic lets your photograph take center stage with remarkable depth and presence. Whether it holds a family legacy or a quiet milestone, this piece gives your memory the foundation it deserves.

Available in Four Heirloom Combinations

Each base is paired with a contrasting perimeter inlay for quiet sophistication.

Walnut + Maple - deep walnut with a bright maple line; warm and dramatic.

Cherry + Walnut - warm cherry with a dark walnut line; rich and quietly tonal.

Cherry + Maple - warm cherry with a pale maple line; bright and soft.

White Oak + Walnut - pale oak with a dark walnut line; clean and modern.

Key Features

  • Two 10mm premium acrylic panels, secured with magnets for easy photo swapping
  • Designed for a 4″×6″ photo to achieve the floating effect; also accommodates a 5″×7″
  • Continuous 1/16″ perimeter inlay on all four sides for a refined, finished edge
  • Intuitive top drop-in mounting channel — no visible hardware
  • Round felt pads for surface protection
  • Hand-rubbed oil finish that deepens the natural grain
