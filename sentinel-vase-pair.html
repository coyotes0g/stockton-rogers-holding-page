The Sentinel Vase Pair

The Sentinel Vase Pair brings quiet sculptural presence and balanced dialogue to any surface. Each vase is hand-turned from premium Northwest Arkansas hardwoods and finished with our signature hand-rubbed oil that deepens the natural grain and offers a warm, living patina over time.

Designed as a coordinated pair, Sentinel I offers a taller, more commanding profile ideal for branches, dried arrangements, or solitary display. Sentinel II provides a lower, grounded counterpoint that anchors the composition. Together they create visual harmony whether placed on a mantel, console table, or open shelf.

Sentinel I & Sentinel II

Sentinel I — The Tall Profile

Taller and more vertical, Sentinel I draws the eye upward. Its elegant taper and generous opening make it perfect for fresh or dried botanicals, or as a standalone sculptural object.

  • Approx. 11–12″ tall
  • Generous 3″ opening
  • Weighted base for stability

Sentinel II — The Grounded Form

Lower and more grounded, Sentinel II offers a sense of quiet strength. Its slightly wider stance and lower center of gravity make it an ideal anchor for the pair or a beautiful vessel on its own.

  • Approx. 7–8″ tall
  • Broader 4.5″ base
  • Perfect counterpoint to Sentinel I
